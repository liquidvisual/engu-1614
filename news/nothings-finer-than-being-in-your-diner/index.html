WHO ARE WE?

We're an Australian Queensland College that's passionate about teaching English. We're engaging, caring, fun and most importantly understand how to maximise your learning experience to the fullest.

  • Apply Today
  • Make an Enquiry

  • Our Mission

    We strive to keep students happy by developing their confidence with great teachers, extensive English programs, facilities and service with uncomplicated policies & exceptional value.

  • Our Teachers

    With teachers and staff from all over the world we're no stranger to living and working abroad. Our friendly staff will offer support with finding a job, improving cultural knowledge of Australia and increasing confidence, accuracy and fluency.

  • Our Location

    Situated in Brisbane Queensland's premier shopping and entertainment precinct, you'll be surrounded by fantastic culture where free events and shows frequently occur throughout the year right at the college's front door!

GO BEYOND THE CLASSROOM

Our primary aims are to deliver dynamic, stimulating, relevant courses to prepare students be be a global citizens in the international world of English.

All Courses

Global Students

Our courses are designed to enable learners to use English independently for global communication, in real-life situations. They provide authentic practice of English and encourage communication skills by presenting structures and skills in an exciting, friendly context. This helps learners improve their English and become better speakers, listeners, readers and writers of this international language.

Global Classrooms

Using a wide range of different methods and the latest technology, our classrooms are full of laughter, movement and fun. These are no ordinary classes! There will also be ample opportunity for leaving the classroom, engaging native speakers in natural English and experiencing the multi-cultural world of Australia.
